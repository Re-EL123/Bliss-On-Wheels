About Us

Welcome to Bliss on Wheels — your sanctuary of relaxation on the move. Founded with the vision of bringing luxury wellness experiences directly to your doorstep, Bliss on Wheels is a premium mobile spa service designed to help you unwind, recharge, and reconnect with yourself, wherever you are. Whether you're at home, in the office, or hosting a special event, we deliver high-quality spa treatments in the comfort and convenience of your chosen space. Our team of trained professionals is passionate about holistic well-being and dedicated to creating a calming, personalized experience for each client. We specialize in a variety of services including therapeutic massages, rejuvenating facials, soothing body treatments, and customized pamper sessions tailored to your needs. At Bliss on Wheels, we believe that self-care is not a luxury — it’s a lifestyle. That’s why we’ve made it our mission to remove the barriers to wellness by providing spa-quality services without the need for travel, traffic, or stress. We come to you, bringing not only top-tier products and expert touch, but also the ambiance and tranquillity of a traditional spa — all in your own space. Discover the bliss of convenience. Discover the bliss of care. Discover Bliss on Wheels.
